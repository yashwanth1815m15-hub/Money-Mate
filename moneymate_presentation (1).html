Money Mate

Smart Expense Tracking & Group Expense Management

Team Members: Yashas, Yashwanth, Lohith
Problem Statement
  • Students and young professionals struggle to track their daily expenses and maintain budgets effectively
  • Managing shared expenses in groups (roommates, trips, events) leads to confusion and conflicts
  • Lack of financial awareness results in overspending and poor savings habits
  • Existing expense tracking apps are either too complex or lack features for group expense management
  • Manual expense tracking using spreadsheets is time-consuming and error-prone
Product/Solution - Innovation
  • Money Mate is a comprehensive expense management application that combines personal finance tracking with intelligent group expense splitting
  • Type: Incremental innovation improving upon existing expense trackers by integrating personal and group expense features seamlessly
  • Key Innovations:
    • AI-powered expense categorization from bill photos
    • Smart group expense splitting with multiple payment methods
    • Real-time expense tracking with instant notifications
    • Budget alerts and spending insights dashboard
  • Uniqueness: First app designed specifically for students combining personal budgeting with hassle-free group expense management in one platform
Product/Solution - Readiness Level
  • Current Stage: Proof of Concept
  • Ideal Customer Profile:
    • College students (18-25 years)
    • Young professionals and entry-level employees
    • Shared accommodation residents
    • Travel groups and social circles
  • Customer Persona: Tech-savvy individuals seeking simple, mobile-first solutions for managing personal and shared expenses
  • Customer Interview Insights:
    • 85% of surveyed students find group expense management challenging
    • 70% prefer mobile apps over manual tracking
    • Users want instant settlement options (UPI integration)
  • Next Phase: Product Requirement Specifications completed, moving towards MVP development

Business Model & Strategy
  • Revenue Model - Freemium with Premium Subscription:
    • Free Tier: Basic expense tracking, up to 5 groups
    • Premium Tier: ₹99/month or ₹999/year
    • Features: Unlimited groups, advanced analytics, export reports, priority support
  • Revenue Forecast:
    • Year 1: 50,000 users, 5% conversion → ₹30 Lakhs
    • Year 2: 150,000 users, 8% conversion → ₹1.2 Crores
    • Year 3: 400,000 users, 10% conversion → ₹4 Crores
  • Business Model: B2C direct-to-consumer through mobile app stores (Google Play, App Store)
  • Additional Revenue: In-app partnerships with financial services (credit cards, loans) for referral commissions
